Policy Significance

Codelist last modified: 2012-03-02T16:37:03.457Z
Codelist Version: 1.0
  • xml
  • csv
  • metadata
codenamedescription
0not targetedThe score "not targeted" means that the activity was examined but found not to target the policy objective.
1significant objectiveSignificant (secondary) policy objectives are those which, although important, were not the prime motivation for undertaking the activity.
2principal objectivePrincipal (primary) policy objectives are those which can be identified as being fundamental in the design and impact of the activity and which are an explicit objective of the activity. They may be selected by answering the question "Would the activity have been undertaken without this objective?"
3principal objective AND in support of an action programmeFor desertification-related aid only
