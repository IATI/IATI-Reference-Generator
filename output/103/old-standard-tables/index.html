Old Standard Overview Tables

These tables have been used by people in the past to help them explore the Activity and Organisation Standards.

However, they are slowly going out of date, and as part of a move to standardise all IATI documentation they are no longer maintained. Links to the documents are retained here for the sake of people that would still like to refer to it, but please note that this website contains the definitive guide to the IATI Standard.
