FSS

<fss></fss>

From the schema

This section allows entry of data required for the OECD DAC Forward Spending Survey at an activity level.

@extraction-date The exact date when the information was collected or extracted from donors' aid management systems.
@priority True if the partner country is a priority partner country.
@phaseout-year If there are plans to phase out operations from the partner country, this column shows the projected year of last disbursements.

FSS

Example Usage

<fss extraction-date="2013-05-31" priority="true" phaseout-year="2016">
    <forecast year="2014" value-date="2013-07-03" currency="GBP">10000</forecast>
    <forecast year="2015" currency="GBP">20000</forecast>
</fss>
