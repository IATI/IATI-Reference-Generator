Codes

Code Name Description
0 not targeted The score “not targeted” means that the activity was examined but found not to target the policy objective.
1 significant objective Significant (secondary) policy objectives are those which, although important, were not the prime motivation for undertaking the activity.
2 principal objective Principal (primary) policy objectives are those which can be identified as being fundamental in the design and impact of the activity and which are an explicit objective of the activity. They may be selected by answering the question “Would the activity have been undertaken without this objective?”
3 principal objective AND in support of an action programme For desertification-related aid only
4 Explicit primary objective  

Guidance

Policy Significance code = 4 (Explicit primary objective) SHOULD ONLY be used in conjunction with Policy Marker code = 9 (Reproductive, Maternal, Newborn and Child Health)
