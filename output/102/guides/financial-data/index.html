Financial Data

IATI captures a wide range of financial information within an activity from overall activity budgets down to transaction level information. This section demonstrates how to report budgets and planned disbursements, and also what is involved in the reporting of transactions , as well as defining transaction types. It also deals with how to report multi-funded activities and how to avoid double counting.
