Technical Advisory Group (TAG)

The Technical Advisory Group is open to any interested organisation or individual, and not restricted to signatories or partner country endorsers of IATI. The functions of the TAG are to discuss and provide proposals for the continued development and maintenance of the Standard. The TAG is managed and overseen by a Technical Advisory Group Secretariat, currently hosted at DIPR, and chaired by Brian Hammond.

TAG Secretariat

As well as managing consultations, and coordinating the TAG, the TAG Secretariat are also responsible for supporting signatory organisations to implement IATI.

The IATI TAG Secretariat are available to offer technical and data support to organisations that are implementing IATI.

Please email Mandy Burrows (mandy.burrows@devinit.org) or post a query in the Knowledge Base if you have any questions or would to arrange a meeting.
