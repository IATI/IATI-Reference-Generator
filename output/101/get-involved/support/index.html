Support

The IATI support service is for individuals and organisations who have questions about:

  • Publishing IATI data
  • Using and understanding IATI data
  • Using the IATI registry

You can search the existing IATI knowledge base by clicking on the blue support tab on the right of the site. The form will also let you submit a question and get a response by email.

You can also visit the support site and knowledge base at: support.iatistandard.org

Anybody can contribute to the knowledge base and we will also update it with answers to the questions we receive by email.
